# Logbook

Append-only history — one entry per AI session, newest alongside oldest. **Low authority,
faithful record.** The canon lives in the cabinet drawers and links back here for provenance.

Entries are filed under `logbook/<year>/<month>/`. A mechanical stub is written at the end of
every session; `bureau:file-session` may **enrich that stub once** (intent, decisions, open
threads) while the session is still in context. After that an entry is append-only — earlier
sessions' entries are never rewritten.
